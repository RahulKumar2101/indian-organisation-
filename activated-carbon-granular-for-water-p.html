Activated Carbon Granular for Water Purification

We Innova Corporate (India), are the leading Manufacturers, Exporters & Suppliers of Activated Carbon Granular for Water Purification.

Activated Carbon Granular is used to adsorb (attach to its internal surfaces) unwanted odours, colours, contaminants of suspended organic matters from gases and liquids. Our products have an extensive network of natural pores with enormous surface area. With our unique processing technology we offer various tailor made grades to suit different applications in the industry as per the following:

  • Activated Carbon Granular for Water Treatment
  • Activated Carbon Granular for Gold Recovery
  • Activated Carbon Granular suitable for Silver Impregnation
  • Activated Carbon Granular for Syrup Solution
  • Activated Carbon Granular for Air and Gas Purification
  • Activated Carbon Granular for Petroleum Refineries
  • Activated Carbon Granular for Effluent Treatment
Activated Carbon Granular for Water Treatment

This granular activated carbon is used for water treatment applications in fixed bed media filters. It is used to remove smell, odour, chlorine, and dissolved organic contaminants from potable and process water. It is also used in DM plant filters, Tap water filters, Desalination plants and Aquariums.

ADVANTAGES :
  • 1. Easy back washing
  • 2. Abundance of micro pores for maximum adsorption
  • 3. High de-chlorination properties compared to coal based carbons
Activated Carbon Granular for GOLD Recovery

Coconut shell based activated carbon granules are widely used for gold recovery in gold mines all over the world. The granules are very hard and free of particles with large pore volume, rapid adsorption and most quick kinetics. This makes the product ideal for gold mines.

ADVANTAGES :
  • 1. Low attrition values
  • 2. Large pore volume with selected pore size
  • 3. Suitable for carbon in leach (CIL) and carbon in pulp (CIP) process
For Silver Impregnation

We are offering granular activated carbons most suitable for silver impregnation.

ADVANTAGES :
  • 1. Efficient adsorption and filling characteristics
  • 2. Suitable pore structure for maximum adsorption
Activated Carbon Granular for Petroleum Refineries

Activated Carbon Granules are used as a catalyst support of macro reactors in petroleum refineries. We supply our products to many reputed refineries in India.

ADVANTAGES :
  • 1. Extremely hard with excellent durability
  • 2. Can be regenerated
Activated Carbon Granular for Air Purification

Deodorisation and air purification is one of the important areas of applications of activated carbon. We offer pure Granular Activated Carbon for the air purification activity. It helps in Refrigerator odour removal, Conditioning of air, Cigarette filters, Kitchen hoods, Gas mask, Purification of industrial and Radio-active gases.

ADVANTAGES :
  • 1. Easy removal of dissolved hydro carbons
  • 2. Highly effective in acidic and basic applications
  • 3. High adsorption
  • 4. Wide applications
  • 5. Can be regenerated
Activated Carbon Granular for Syrup Solutions

Soft Drinks and Beverage industries are also using activated carbon granular for the following application in syrup solutions.

  • 1. Used for Decolourisation of Sweeteners in Syrup Solution
  • 2. Used in liquor treatment, like improving the taste of liquors
  • 3. Removal of colloidal matters from soft drinks
ADVANTAGES :
  • 1. Low ash and dust.
  • 2. Selected pore structure: excellent decolourisation
Activated Carbon Granular for Effluent Treatment

Activated carbons in effluent treatment process are used for removal of colour and odour from waste water.

ADVANTAGES :
  • 1. Removal of dissolved organic matter
  • 2. Cost effective in eliminating of pesticides & pollutants
  • 3. Excellent decoloring
