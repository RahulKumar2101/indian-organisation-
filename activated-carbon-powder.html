Activated Carbon Powder

We Innova Corporate (India), are one of the Largest Manufacturer, Supplier & Exporter of Activated Carbon Powder, Powder Activated Carbon based in New Delhi, India.

Activated Carbon Powder is manufactured from PINE WOOD and from other raw materials / natural sources also. PINE WOOD based Charcoal is soft, have a high degree of porosity, higher surface area, high purity and therefore gives better result compared with other Carbon.

Activated Carbon powder manufacturers

WASHED ACTIVATED CARBON POWDER contains less Iron, Ash, Acid & Water Soluble Matter. It passes through I.P., B.P., & U.S.P. parameters. So, it is quite suitable for Bulk Drug Industries etc.

UNWASHED ACTIVATED CARBON POWDER contains high Iron, Ash, Acid & Water Soluble Matter compared to Washed Activated Carbon Powder. So, it is quite suitable for Dyes & Intermediates Industries, Fine Chemicals etc.
