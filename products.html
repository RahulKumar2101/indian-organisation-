Our Products

Innova Corporate (India), a leading name in the Carbon Sector, is proud to offer qualitative Activated Carbon Granular & Activated Carbon Powder as part of its product range. The entire product range offered by us is processed using superior quality.

Widely used in numerous industries for air-adsorption applications, our Activated Carbon Granular & Activated Carbon Powder has helped us to carve our niche in markets across the globe.

Known for quality and precision, our Activated Carbon Granular & Activated Carbon Powder has been extensively used for purification in markets for any type of adsorption applications. Clients are also pleased at the easy availability of Activated Carbon Granular & Activated Carbon Powder at competitive pricing and the premium services we offer.

Our Activated carbon products are made from selected shells of coconut to ensure the highest quality during the manufacturing process. By maintaining high quality control standards, the manufacturer is able to produce high-grade coconut shell carbon impurities. Coconut carbon is especially effective in removing trace organic compounds such as pesticides.

We have the capability and flexibility to offer products, such as particle size distribution, as required for this application. Our products have the following advantages over many other types of carbon:

  • High hardness levels and low dust levels. This improves material handling characteristics and is especially important where carbon fines cannot be tolerated.
  • High surface area, up to 1400 m²/g. This allows for long life and high adsorption capacity.
  • High retentivity, preventing unwanted desorption of adsorbed species.
  • Large fraction of micropores (> 20 Angstroms). This is important for removal of low-molecular-weight organics and for removal of trace levels of contaminants.
  • Low ash and fewer impurities.

Coconut shell activated carbons are ideal for gas-phase applications and for liquid-phase applications where purification to trace concentrations is required. Activated carbon manufactured by us are used in many domestic and industrial applications like:

In Water & Liquid Applications
  • Municipal water treatment - taste, odour and micro pollutant removal.
  • Domestic water treatment - in-line and cartridge filters
  • Industrial and hospital water treatment - Reduction of total organic halogens (TOX), biological oxygen demand (BOD) and chemical oxygen demand (COD) and dechlorination.
  • Ground water treatment - Reduction of total organic halogens (TOX) and adsorbable organic halogens (AOX)
  • Purification of edible oils and fats, alcoholic and soft drinks etc.
  • Gold mines for adsorption of metal and cyanide complexes from Carbon-in-pulp (CIP), Carbon-in-column (CIL) and heap leach circuits.
  • Swimming pools - for removal of residual ozone and control of chloramine levels.
In Air & Gas Applications
  • Air purification and environmental protection - Removal of solvents and hydrocarbons, deodorisation, air conditioning, fume traps etc.
  • Cleaning process gases - Removal of contaminants from hydrogen, natural gas, carbon dioxide, solvent recovery etc.
  • Protection equipments - Gas masks, removal of harmful or toxic compounds, cigarette filters etc etc.
